ABOUT SHINE PUBLIC SCHOOL PURNEA

SHINE PUBLIC SCHOOL PURNEA was established in 2011 and it is managed by the Unrecognised. It is located in Urban area. It is located in PURNIA block of PURNIA district of Bihar. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
